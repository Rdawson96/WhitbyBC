Who are we?

We are Whitby Beach Clean, a community group of volunteers who care about the environment and the beauty of our beaches. We organize regular meet ups to collect litter, sort recyclables and raise awareness about the importance of keeping our coastlines clean and healthy.

Our Impact So Far

Since we started in 2020, we have collected over 10 tons of waste from the beaches of Whitby, including plastic bottles, fishing nets, cigarette butts and more. All this helps us make our shores safer for wildlife and more enjoyable for visitors. We have also donated some of the recyclables to local charities and schools, and created art projects with them. Our group is not only helping the environment, but also building friendships and connections among people who share a common passion.

Where to find us

Follow us on social media or contact us by email to find out more about us and what we do or if you have any questions or suggestions. We hope to see you soon!
