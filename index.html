Who are we?

Welcome to Whitby Beach Clean, a community group of volunteers who care about the environment and the beauty of our beaches. We organize regular meet ups to collect litter, sort recyclables and raise awareness about the importance of keeping our coastlines clean and healthy.

Our Impact So Far

Since we started in 2020, we have collected over 10 tons of waste from the beaches of Whitby, making them safer for wildlife and more enjoyable for visitors. We have also donated some of the recyclables to local charities and schools, and created art projects with them. Our group is not only helping the environment, but also building friendships and connections among people who share a common passion.
