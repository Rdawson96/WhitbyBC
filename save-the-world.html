Help us Save the World!

Reduce plastic consumption

The production of plastic contributes to air pollution, and discarded plastic items can take hundreds of years to decompose. By reducing our plastic usage, we can decrease both the demand for plastic and the amount of plastic waste that ends up in our oceans. This can be as simple as using a reusable water bottle instead of buying bottled water, or bringing your own bags to the grocery store instead of using the store's plastic bags.

Make Sure to recycle properly

While recycling is not a cure-all solution, it can help reduce the amount of new plastic being produced. However, it's important to recycle properly, as incorrect recycling can lead to contamination, making the entire batch of recyclables unprocessable. Make sure to clean out your recyclables before putting them in the bin, and check your local recycling guidelines to see what types of plastic are accepted.

Spread awareness

Many people are unaware of the extent of plastic pollution and its impact on marine life. By educating others, you can help spread awareness and inspire others to take action. This could be through social media, local community meetings, schools, or even casual conversations with friends and family.
